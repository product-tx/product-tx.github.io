Product@TX

We provide students mentorship and guidance to land internships at top companies in the tech industry in product management. Any major/year can apply to learn from our mentors that have previously navigated the recruiting process for many companies.

Applications for the 2022 cohort open on Feb 28th and close on March 11th.

Our organization was founded by students who survived and eventually conquered the recruiting gauntlet, receiving offers from Uber, Twitter, Intuit, Microsoft, Dropbox, Indeed, and many more. Regardless of which career path we chose, we all felt as though we couldn't have gotten where we are without the help of others -- help that we just happened to receive due to chance meetings, our own personal networks, and other similar one-off event. We each recognized that we are lucky, and felt the need to expand access to the resources we used to achieve our career goals (coaching, mentorship, and teaching chief among them).

As a member of Product@TX, you will have access to networking events with PM recruiters who want to inform & interact with UT students, opportunities to work with APMs/PMs to restructure your resume to focus on PM-relevant experiences & attributes, and gain exposure to PMs at different companies to get a better idea of what the companies are like and what PM is like at that company. Product@tx provides students with mentorship as they apply to companies, search for jobs, and want to learn more about PM in general.
